About the event

AIACPA's Annual Education Seminar will be held on Sunday, April 24th, 2016, at the Radisson Hotel & Convention Center, Camp Hill, PA. The seminar will start from 8:30 AM to 1 PM, followed by lunch and networking.

The seminar is open to all (no children below 12 years of age) and free including lunch.

The seminar will focus on Family Health - Physical, Psychological, Work Stress and Survival. Subject matter experts will cover health issues for all age groups. The seminar will also contain useful information about health management, hospitalization procedures & processes, and management of financial burden.
