About the event

India Day is one of the most well attended events in the Harrisburg area where all Asian Indian communities come together to present the rich Indian culture and heritage. Over the past many years, Asian Indian Americans of Central PA (AIACPA) has been organizing this event at the Forum auditorium in Harrisburg attracting audience from all over Central Pennsylvania. India Day has become the most awaited event for our community, organizers, Asian an organizations, participants, sponsors,vendors and our audience. In the past the events have been enthusiastically attended by well-known local, state and national politicians.
