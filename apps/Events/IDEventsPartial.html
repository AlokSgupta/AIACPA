About the event

India Day - a Community Fair includes live stage entertainment, variety of ethnic food, Indian apparel and jewelry vendors, ample open and indoor dinning and socializing area, fashion parade, Garba, Bhangra dances in the evening, open green space to stroll, fair (mela) atmosphere. Mouthwatering dishes will include live cooking of Jalebis, Dosas, Chole Bhaturas and barbecued items like paneer, chicken, shish kebabs, etc.

Getting Involved in India Day

AIACPA is always looking for participants, performers, choreographers, coordinators, Emcees, and all volunteers in making this community program a huge success. Being part of this event provides you an exposure to over 10,000 to 15,000 audience and a prestigious recognition from your community and AIA.
